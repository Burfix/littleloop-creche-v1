# LittleLoop Creche App

## Product Positioning

LittleLoop replaces broad WhatsApp groups and manual receipt books with a private, mobile-first creche portal for parents, teachers, and the owner.

The Project Goose / projectgumpo.space positioning has several strong ideas worth borrowing: verified staff updates, guardian-only access, QR check-in/out, POPIA-aware privacy, school/parent audience paths, and hands-on onboarding.

The app should feel warm to parents, fast for teachers, and financially clear for the owner.

## Core Promise

Parents open the app because they want to see their child's day.
Teachers use it because it saves time.
The owner keeps it because it improves billing, records, and trust.

## User Roles

### Parents

- Private child photos and daily moments
- Verified daily mood, meal, nap, and activity summary
- Live child status: in care, picked up, or not arrived
- Timeline of check-in, meals, naps, incidents, activities, and photos
- Invoices, balances, and digital receipts
- Event reminders and supply notices
- Pickup alerts and approved guardian information
- Milestone albums that become a memory book over time
- Secure QR check-in/out visibility

### Teachers

- One-tap attendance
- Quick photo capture and child tagging
- Meal, nap, toilet, and mood updates
- Nap timer, meal logging, attendance, and incident reporting as fast actions
- Notes to individual parents or the whole class
- Auto-generated day summary from captured moments
- QR pickup checks and allergy/emergency visibility

### Owner/Admin

- Dashboard for paid, overdue, and expected fees
- Invoice and receipt management
- Parent and child records
- Consent tracking for photos and communication
- Guardian-only access controls
- Staff activity overview
- Reports for attendance, payments, and enrolment
- Guided onboarding for rooms, roles, staff, and parent invites

## Engagement Loops

The app should be habit-forming because it is useful and emotionally valuable, not because it pressures people.

### Daily Moment Loop

Teacher captures a few moments during the day.
The update is verified by staff and time-stamped.
Parent receives a gentle notification.
Parent opens the app to see a private update.
The moment is saved into the child's album.

### Check-In Confidence Loop

Parent or guardian scans a unique QR at drop-off or pickup.
The system records the action with a timestamp and signature.
Staff and parents both see the latest status.
The owner has an audit trail if there is ever a dispute.

### Payment Confidence Loop

Invoice is issued.
Parent gets a reminder.
Payment is marked or confirmed.
Receipt appears instantly.
Owner sees updated cash flow.

### Memory Loop

Moments collect into monthly albums.
Parents can see milestones and growth.
At term end, the app can generate a memory book or report.

### Trust Loop

Parents know their child's photos are not being shared with every family.
The creche can show that consent, access, and records are handled professionally.

### Onboarding Loop

The app is sold with setup help, not just software.
Rooms, classes, staff roles, child profiles, and parent invites are configured with the creche.
This makes adoption easier for non-technical staff.

## MVP Scope

Build the first version around the highest pain points:

1. Parent login
2. Child profiles
3. Private photo moments
4. Notices
5. QR check-in/out
6. Parent daily timeline
7. Manual payment tracking
8. Digital receipts
9. Admin dashboard

Online card payments, advanced reports, and memory books can come later.

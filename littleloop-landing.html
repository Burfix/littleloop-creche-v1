How it works

Set up in a day.
Used every day.

LittleLoop is designed for creche owners who don't have time for complexity.

01

You onboard your school

We set up your school in minutes. Add your classes, invite your teachers and parents — they get an email and set their own password.

02

Teachers run the day

Check children in, log meals, mood, and nap time. Post moments. Tick off tasks. All from their phone, in real time.

03

Parents stay connected

Every parent sees their child's full day as it happens — no waiting for a WhatsApp message or a paper note at pickup.
